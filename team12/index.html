Our Creations

We are making Fable-Holders and Twinkle Lights. Fable-Holders are bookmarks, and Twinkle Lights are paper creations on fairy lights.

Fable-Holders

Our products are colorful and uniquely shaped laminated bookmarks. There are many different types of fable-holders to match the story. These bookmarks help keep your book in good condition, and they help keep your book pages straight. Fable-Holders come in different designs and colors. (Click the title to open the pictures!)

Twinkle Lights

Twinkle Lights are origami creations strung on fairy lights. They provide calming ambient light, perfect for a bedroom or study. Twinkle Lights are around 3'3 ft long and come in different colors and designs. (Click the title to open the pictures!)

We are planning to sell the Twinkle Lights for 8-10 dollars and the Fable-Holders for around 3 dollars. There would be 10 of each of the three Twinkle Light designs, and also 10 of each of the three Fable-Holder designs. (So thirty Twinklies and thirty Fable-Holders.)
