Welcome to Flor

Flor means flower in Spanish. Beautiful and delicate, just like our cards. From the birthday card to the get well card, all of these cards were hand-made, including our own digital art. Our mission is to spread joy through cards. We want people to smile when they receive one. We want to enocurage connection and building relationships with kind notes and fun designs that make people laugh.
