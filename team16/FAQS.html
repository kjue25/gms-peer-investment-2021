How much do the wraps cost?

We are selling them for around 6 dollars each (depending on the size) and there will probably be discounts on packs.

How do you clean them?

Our wraps can be easily cleaned with cold to warm water (hot water might melt the wax) and a washcloth. You just need to wipe them down and then they are ready to use again!

Are they food safe?

Yes. All of the ingredients are edible (though we donâ€™t recommend eating them) and your food is safe! However you cannot wash them with boiling water, so do not use them on raw meat.

What are in them?

Beeswax, jojoba oil, and pine resin.

Do they have a smell?

They do not have a particularly strong scent, but they do smell a little bit like honey (because of the beeswax)

Are the bags sealable?

Yes, they keep a pretty strong seal so your food won't fall out. However don't carry liquids in them (for obvious reasons)

Do they stick to bowls well?

Yes, you can lift a corner to check in a bowl without the whole thing coming off.
