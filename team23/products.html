FAQ

What are your stickers made of?

Our stickers are made out of very durable vinyl.


How big are your stickers?

Our stickers are all around or under 3 inches by 3 inches, varying by design.


Are your stickers waterproof?

Yes! We spray each sticker with acrylic spray to make them waterproof and long-lasting.


Are your stickers scratch-resistant?

Yes! We spray each sticker with acrylic spray to make them scratch-resistant and long-lasting.


What made you decide on making stickers?

We decided on stickers because in these trying times, we’ve realized that people need things to add just a little bit of happiness to their lives, no matter the size! Hence our product, stickers.


Where did you get your company name from?

We got the idea for our company from a whole bunch of things! We wanted a name that could represent our wide variety of designs while also sounding swag, so, Swag Stickers was born!
