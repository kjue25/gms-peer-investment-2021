Alika Earrings

Hi! Welcome to Alika Earrings! We make beautiful earrings that are super cute and a add a unique touch to your outfit. If you don't have your ears pierced you can gift these earrings to a parent or friend! We hand craft our earrings from the pouring of the resin to the molding of the clay, we make sure to produce our best possible handmade earrings for your ears. We also use hypoallergenic metals like titanium and sterling silver so that YOU can wear our earrings! :)
