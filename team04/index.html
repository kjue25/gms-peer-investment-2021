The Beaded Collection

We are The Beaded Collection, and we sell beaded necklaces and bracelets. Our products have cool glass beads, but they are as durable or more more than regular beads. We also have an assortment of amazing colors! They look great with school uniforms and other clothes as well! The bracelets come with hand beaded flowers on them. We hand make all of our products and we are really passionate about our project.
